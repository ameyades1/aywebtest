The Chosen Palette

Version 7
White Copper

Copper · Gold · Parchment-White

Copper is the metal of Indian ritual — kalash vessels, temple bells, puja thalis passed down through generations. Gold is the colour of spiritual illumination and the Vedic fire ceremony.

Paper-white evokes the palm-leaf manuscripts that carried ancient knowledge across centuries. Together they form a palette immediately familiar to anyone rooted in Indian spiritual life — clean and legible on screen, timeless in meaning.
