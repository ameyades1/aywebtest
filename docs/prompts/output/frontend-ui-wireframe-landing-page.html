Meet the Master

Swami Anandarishi

Swami Anandarishi has dedicated over three decades to the practice and teaching of classical yoga and meditation. His profound insights into the ancient sciences of inner transformation have guided thousands on the path to self-discovery.

Drawing from the timeless wisdom of yoga, tantra, and vedanta, the Master offers teachings that are both deeply rooted in tradition and remarkably relevant to modern life. His approach emphasizes direct experience over dogma, inviting seekers to discover truth within themselves.

AntarYog Foundation exists to make the transformative science of yoga accessible to all seekers. Through authentic teachings, powerful practices, and a global community of consciousness, we are fostering a world where inner well-being is the foundation for outer harmony.

From intimate satsangs to large-scale retreats, from local community programs to global online initiatives, our mission is to serve humanity's evolution toward higher consciousness.
