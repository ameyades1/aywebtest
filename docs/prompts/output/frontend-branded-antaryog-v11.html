Mission & Purpose

Awakening the Inner Self

AntarYog Foundation stands at the confluence of ancient Vedantic wisdom and the urgent need of our modern age. Founded by Jeevan Mukt Sadguru Acharya Upendra Ji — a realized master with over 25 years of dedicated service — the Foundation creates transformative pathways for seekers across Bharat and the world.

“When the inner is purified, the outer world transforms — this is the eternal law of Vedanta.”
— Acharya Upendra Ji

The Foundation’s approach is rooted in the living tradition of Guru-Shishya Parampara. Programs range from immersive Gurukul residencies to community Shibirs, each designed to initiate genuine transformation in the practitioner’s daily life, relationships, and understanding of Self.

With an unwavering vision of making Bharat a Vishwa Guru, AntarYog Foundation weaves together the ancient sciences of Yoga, Vedanta, Naadi Jyotish, Ayurveda, and Vastu into an integrated curriculum of inner development.

Our Three Pillars

The Vision of AntarYog

◆

Inner Transformation

Every program at AntarYog is ultimately about the individual’s inner journey. Drawing from Vedanta, Yoga, and the Guru-Shishya tradition, we guide seekers toward direct experience of the Self.

◆

Community & Sangha

Transformation deepens in conscious community. AntarYog cultivates a global Sangha of sincere seekers united by the pursuit of truth, service, and the revival of Vedantic culture in daily life.

◆

Bharat as Vishwa Guru

The highest aim: to ignite within Bharat the light of spiritual leadership that can guide humanity. AntarYog Foundation trains teachers, healers, and leaders rooted in Vedantic dharma for global service.
