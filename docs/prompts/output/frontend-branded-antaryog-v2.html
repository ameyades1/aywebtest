What is Antar Yog?

A Transformative Journey Within

Antar Yog is a comprehensive spiritual framework that integrates the timeless wisdom of Vedanta, Yoga, and ancient Indian scriptures to facilitate profound inner transformation.

Under the divine guidance of Acharya Upendra Ji, a Jeevan Mukt Sadguru, we are reviving the eternal principles of Sanatan Dharma to create a harmonious, enlightened society.

Our mission encompasses multiple dimensions: spiritual education, holistic healing, yogic practices, Vedic sciences like Naadi Jyotish and Vastu Rupantaran, and the revival of the Gurukul educational system.

Our Vision
