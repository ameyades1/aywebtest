Our Vision

जनहिताय स्वयम् मोक्षाय च

Under the leadership of Acharya Upendra Ji, Antar Yog Foundation aims to restore the identity of Bharat as a "Vishwa Guru": a healthy, wealthy and spiritual guide of the world. We see a world where every person: whether seeking health, wealth, relationships, or inner peace: discovers their true self, living life not as a human searching for the divine, but a divine being joyfully experiencing the human journey. Antar Yog is a path that does not ask you to leave your world behind but transform it from inside out.


Guided by the principle of "जनहिताय स्वयम् मोक्षाय च", we envision everyone achieving their own liberation while serving for the welfare of all.


To serve the world, every person needs power, wealth, wisdom, and leadership: all at once. This can only be learnt in the Gurukul: A world-renowned character-building education system where students are taught both spiritual and worldly knowledge.


We see Bharat once again becoming the world's spiritual and intellectual leader, not through force, but through the irresistible power of the enlightened example. Under Bharat's leadership, the world transitions from a state of conflict to peace and harmony.

Reviving timeless knowledge of ancient scriptures under the guidance of Jeevan Mukt Sadguru Acharya Upendra Ji.
