Meet the Master

Jeevan Mukt Sadguru
Acharya Upendra Ji

A liberated master living in the world, Acharya Upendra Ji is a beacon of Vedantic wisdom for our age. His life is a living testament to what becomes possible when ancient knowledge meets sincere sadhana.

With profound mastery in Vedanta, Yoga, Naadi Jyotish, and ancient healing sciences, he guides thousands on the path of self-realization and inner transformation.

His vision: to re-establish Bharat as the world's spiritual teacher — Vishwa Guru — by awakening Vedantic consciousness in each seeker who enters the sacred space of AntarYog Foundation.
