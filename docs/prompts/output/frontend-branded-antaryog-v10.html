Meet Acharya Upendra Ji

A liberated master living in the world, Acharya Upendra Ji is a beacon of Vedantic wisdom. With over 25 years of dedicated service, he guides thousands on the path of self-realization and genuine inner transformation.

His vision: to re-establish Bharat as Vishwa Guru — the world's spiritual teacher — by awakening Vedantic consciousness in each individual through the integrated practices of Yoga, Naadi Jyotish, Vastu, Ayurveda, and the Gurukul system.

AntarYog Foundation stands at the confluence of timeless ancient wisdom and the urgent need of our age, creating transformative pathways for seekers across Bharat and the world.
