Vision
Under the leadership of Acharya Upendra Ji, Antar Yog Foundation aims to restore the identity of Bharat as a "Vishwa Guru": a healthy, wealthy and spiritual guide of the world. We see a world where every person: whether seeking health, wealth, relationships, or inner peace: finds their path back to their true self, living life not as a human searching for the divine, but a divine being joyfully experiencing the human journey. Antar Yog is a path that does not ask you to leave your world behind but transform it from inside out.
Guided by the principle of "जनहिताय स्वयम् मोक्षाय च", we envision everyone achieving their own liberation while serving for the welfare of all.
To serve the world, every person needs power, wealth, wisdom, and leadership: all at once. This can only be learnt in the Gurukul: A world-renowned character-building education system where students are taught both spiritual and worldly knowledge.
We see Bharat once again becoming the world's spiritual and intellectual leader, not through force, but through the irresistible power of the enlightened example. Under Bharat's leadership, the world transitions from a state of conflict to peace and harmony.

Mission
Transforming minds, transforming lives, transforming the nation.

Youth Leadership
 - Revive the Gurukul system to develop legendary leaders with the Guru-Shishya tradition.
 - Empower women through knowledge of Advaita Vedanta to create self-reliant, independent and compassionate leaders capable of transforming society.

Personal Growth
 - Deliver "Now-and-Here" results for better health, wealth and relationships using ancient spiritual tools and Sadhanas.
 - Create financially free seekers who pursue larger-than-life goals using ethical wealth creation methods.
 - Spread ancient wisdom hidden in scriptures by decoding their profound symbolic meaning useful in everyday life.

Healthy Society
 - Integrate modern science with ancient healing practices to reveal the true root cause of disease with spiritual knowledge.
 - Promote Yogic farming to ensure disease-free citizens and prosperous farmers.

Societal Transformation
 - Re-establish the ancient Varna system based on qualities and actions rather than birth to help citizens rise and eradicate the modern caste system.
 - Unite spiritual movements and sects into one single powerful garland of Sanatan Dharma to strengthen our nation.
 - Teach the righteous way of donation with unconditional love for humanitarian service to the needy.
