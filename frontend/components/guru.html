Acharya Upendra Ji

The founder of Antar Yog, Acharya Upendra Ji, is a Jeevan Mukt Sadguru. Since 2005, he is tirelessly working towards the vision of making Bharat a Vishwa Guru and a Superpower. He is a master of Tantra Vidya and has composed powerful sadhanas through years of deep meditation and penance. A master healer, a Shree Vidya Guru, an entreprenuer and author of over 60+ books, he is an infinite source of wisdom. He holds two degrees in Engineering.
