Vision

Ancient India rose to prosperity and greatness through the divine wisdom of Vedanta and the Guru–Shishya tradition. Antar Yog envisions a healthy, prosperous, and awakened India that once again becomes a Vishwa Guru — a guide for the world. We seek to restore this wisdom as a foundation of life for a balanced relationship between leaders and citizens.

Our India will be a harmonious society where all faiths live together based on eternal principles of truth, compassion, duty, and unity. We aim to re-establish a balanced social system based on a person's qualities and actions, not birth. Antar Yog is dedicated to awakening the Durga Shakti (inner strength) within women. Both men and women must play a strong role in building families, society, and the nation.

This vision comes alive only when the youth dedicate themselves to selfless service of the world. Antar Yog seeks to nurture dynamic leaders, especially women, who transform society with compassion, self-reliance and independence.

True change begins with the mind. With inner peace, families heal, society becomes harmonious and nations become wise. As millions realize their true self, a healthy, prosperous, and spiritual India emerges — once again guiding the world toward peace, knowledge, and humanity.

"This is not just an aspiration, but the responsibility of every generation."
Our Mission

  • Empower women through knowledge of Advaita Vedanta to create self-reliant, independent and compassionate leaders capable of transforming society.
02

Personal Growth

  • Deliver "Now-and-Here" results for better health, wealth and relationships using ancient spiritual tools and Sadhanas.

03

Healthy Society

  • Integrate modern science with ancient healing practices to reveal the true root cause of disease with spiritual knowledge.
  • Promote Yogic farming to ensure disease-free citizens and prosperous farmers.
04

Societal Transformation

  • Re-establish the ancient Varna system based on qualities and actions rather than birth to help citizens rise and eradicate the modern caste system.
  • Unite spiritual movements and sects into one single powerful garland of Sanatan Dharma to strengthen our nation.
  • Teach the righteous way of donation with unconditional love for humanitarian service to the needy.
