The Foundation

Building the Path

Driven by the ancient principle of जनहिताय स्वयं मोक्षाय च — welfare of all and liberation of self — Acharya Ji founded Antar Yog Foundation to make the powerful sadhanas accessible to everyone. Through Shibirs (immersive spiritual camps), Discourses, the residential Gurukul, and Nirjanwas retreats, he has personally guided over 10,000 seekers across 50+ cities.

Acharya Ji is deeply committed to the awakening of women, honoring the Durga Shakti — divine feminine power — within every person. He teaches that true national transformation begins when both men and women rise as compassionate, self-reliant leaders grounded in inner wisdom.

The Vision

Reviving Ancient Wisdom

Beyond Vedanta, he revives classical Indian knowledge systems including Naadi Jyotish (ancient predictive astrology encoded on palm leaves), Vastu Rupantaran (the science of harmonizing living spaces with cosmic energy), and the healing traditions preserved in the scriptures. He teaches these not as rituals, but as living sciences rooted in universal principles.

His ultimate vision is a transformed Bharat — a nation of awakened, prosperous, and spiritually grounded citizens — that once again rises as a Vishwa Guru, a guiding light for all of humanity. Every program, every teaching, every Shibir is one step toward this great aspiration.
